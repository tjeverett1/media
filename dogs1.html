• Bring Me Back

Meet Luna, our resident snuggle champion. This morning she insisted on being tucked into her favorite cashmere throw—so much so that she looked like the cutest little burrito you’ve ever seen. As she peeks out with those round, curious eyes, it’s impossible not to smile. Whether she’s dreaming of squirrel chases or plotting her next treat pursuit, wrapped up like this she’s pure contentment wrapped in fur. Life is definitely cozier with a “blanket burrito” by your side!
